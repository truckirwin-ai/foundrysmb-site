Acceptable Use.

Last updated · April 30, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of the Foundry SMB website, the Foundry Toolkit, advisory engagements, coaching services, and all related content (collectively, the "Services"). It is incorporated into the Terms of Service.

Prohibited activities

You agree not to use the Services to:

  • Violate any applicable law or regulation.
  • Infringe the intellectual property, privacy, or other rights of any third party.
  • Resell, sublicense, redistribute, or publicly post Foundry SMB content (including playbooks, prompts, templates, recordings, and agent configurations) without written permission.
  • Use Foundry SMB content as training data for competing products or models.
  • Reverse-engineer, decompile, or disassemble any software or systems we provide.
  • Misrepresent your identity or your relationship with Foundry SMB.
  • Send spam, malware, or harmful code; probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of any system.
  • Harass, threaten, or harm any person, including Foundry SMB staff, members, or coaching peers.
  • Use the Services to develop or operate weapons, surveillance against individuals without consent, or applications intended to cause harm.
  • Use the Services in any manner that could damage, disable, overburden, or impair them.

Community conduct

The Foundry community is a working space for operators. Direct disagreement is welcome. Personal attacks, harassment, discriminatory speech, and bad-faith behavior are not. Foundry SMB may remove content and suspend or terminate access for community-conduct violations.

AI-specific terms

Foundry SMB content includes prompts, agent configurations, and methodologies designed to be used with third-party AI systems. You are responsible for complying with the terms of the AI providers you use. You are also responsible for human review of AI output before relying on it for decisions that affect customers, employees, or finances.

Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings, content removal, suspension, or termination of your account, with or without refund, in our sole discretion. We may report serious violations to law enforcement.

Reporting

To report a violation: support@foundrysmb.com.
