# AI Agent Operating Workflow

## Purpose

Use AI agents to create cash-producing inputs: leads, offers, assets, outreach, content, and product concepts. Reject work that does not feed sales or product creation.

## Daily operating cadence

1. Pick one vertical.
2. Find pain signals.
3. Turn pain into one offer angle.
4. Draft outreach and content.
5. Build one reusable asset.
6. Score everything against cash proximity.

## Agent roles

### Market Scout

- Input: target vertical, geography, service category.
- Output: lead list, pain signals, public workflow clues, common offers.
- Daily task: find 10 lead candidates and 5 pain patterns.
- Test: each lead has a plausible workflow pain and decision-maker path.

### Offer Builder

- Input: pain pattern, buyer role, workflow type.
- Output: landing copy block, offer promise, risk reversal, objections.
- Daily task: create 3 sprint angles from Market Scout findings.
- Test: a cold buyer can understand the offer in 10 seconds.

### Asset Builder

- Input: selected offer angle and workflow.
- Output: worksheet, SOP, checklist, prompt pack, calculator, or template.
- Daily task: draft one asset that can be used in sales or delivery.
- Test: the asset can be sent to a prospect or used on a call today.

### Outreach Researcher

- Input: lead list and offer angle.
- Output: lead briefs and personalized first lines.
- Daily task: prepare 20 first-touch messages.
- Test: every message references a real business clue.

### Content Repurposer

- Input: sales asset, workflow teardown, or sprint result.
- Output: LinkedIn post, short video script, email, and lead magnet CTA.
- Daily task: create 3 content pieces from one asset.
- Test: every piece points to sprint, toolkit, or email capture.

### POD Tester

- Input: niche, buyer identity, phrase bank, visual direction.
- Output: product concepts, listing titles, validation checklist.
- Daily task: draft 5 POD concepts for validation.
- Test: each concept has a named buyer and search intent.

## Cash proximity score

- 3: Can produce a lead, reply, booked call, paid sprint, or shippable product today.
- 2: Improves an asset used in sales or delivery this week.
- 1: Interesting but not tied to a current offer.
- 0: Busywork. Kill it.

Only 2s and 3s survive the daily review.
