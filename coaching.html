C.2 · What happens

Every session ends with a decision or an artifact.

  1. Priority review

    What matters this week, what is stuck, what must be decided.

  2. Workflow critique

    Review prompts, SOPs, automations, data inputs, and team handoffs.

  3. Asset refinement

    Tighten templates, messages, sales assets, and implementation checklists.

  4. Accountability

    Track action items, blockers, and next proof point.
