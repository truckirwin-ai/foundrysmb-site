- Sprint readiness checklist

## Demo script outline

1. Pick one recurring workflow.
2. List every handoff and tool.
3. Estimate time lost and deal impact.
4. Ask the prompt pack to identify automation and assistant candidates.
5. Score the workflow against pain, repetition, and authority.
6. Decide: self-install, buy toolkit, or book sprint.
