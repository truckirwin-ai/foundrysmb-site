- PDF guide: "Build the Context Vault"

## Narrow problem solved

Most AI work fails because the assistant lacks the business context. This pack creates a practical vault for policies, offers, clients, projects, voice, examples, and rules.

## Demo script outline

1. Inventory the current knowledge sources.
2. Create the vault folders.
3. Fill the business facts document.
4. Add three examples of good work.
5. Create the first SOP from an expert walkthrough.
6. Test an assistant against the vault.
