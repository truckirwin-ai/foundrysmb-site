The Foundry maker’s marks.

Ten circular stamps. Cast-iron field. Molten signature on every badge. Bold cream geometry inside.

Concept

Maker’s marks. Cast in iron.

Each icon is a foundry stamp. Dark cast-iron field, thin incandescent ring, bold cream symbol inside, and a molten signature point at the bottom of every badge — the foundry signature. Designed to read at first glance and to feel like something physical, not pixel-thin.

Construction: 64×64 grid. Symbol fills 60% of the badge. Stroke weight 3px on linear elements. Filled silhouettes wherever a symbol can carry mass without losing clarity. The molten yellow is reserved for the heat moment in each icon — the apex, the core, the spark, the pour.
