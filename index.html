02 · The pain

The money is hiding in the work nobody wants to touch.

The bids nobody wants to assemble. The follow-ups that drift. The reports built by copy-paste. The SOPs trapped in someone's head. The lead list that never becomes calls.

That is not an AI education problem. It is an operating system problem. Foundry SMB starts with one expensive workflow, maps the economic leak, and builds the first AI-assisted version fast enough to prove whether it deserves more investment.

  • Proposal and bid assembly taking days instead of hours.
  • Manual lead triage, research, and follow-up wasting sales time.
  • Reports, summaries, and client updates built from scattered notes.
  • Institutional knowledge stuck in documents, inboxes, and memory.
