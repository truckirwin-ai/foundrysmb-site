# POD Validation Pack

POD is a sidecar experiment, not the primary cash engine. The service funnel gets priority.

## Niches

1. Colorado operator life.

3. Overlanding, FJ60, and adventure operator identity.

## Validation checklist

- Buyer identity is obvious.
- Phrase sounds like the niche, not generic AI output.
- Design can work in one or two colors.
- Search terms exist on Etsy, Shopify, TikTok, Pinterest, or Google.
- Product has a gift angle or identity angle.
- Listing can be tested without building a full brand.
- No trademark risk.
- No copyrighted vehicle logos or protected marks.
- Mockup looks good on shirt, sticker, hat, or mug.
- Kill if nobody can name who would wear it.
