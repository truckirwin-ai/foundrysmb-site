Privacy Policy.

Last updated · April 30, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Foundry SMB LLC ("Foundry SMB," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit foundrysmb.com or use our services. By using the site or services, you agree to this Policy.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide. Name, email address, business name, billing address, payment information (processed by Stripe), and any other information you submit through forms, email, or during an engagement.

Automatically collected. IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referring URL, and timestamps. Collected via standard server logs and cookies.

From third parties. Limited information from payment processors (e.g., transaction confirmation, last four digits of card) and authentication providers if you use them.

3. How we use information

To deliver and operate the services, process payments, provide customer support, communicate with you about your account or our services, comply with legal obligations, prevent fraud, and improve our products and content. We do not sell personal information.

4. Legal bases (for users in the EU/UK)

We process personal data under one or more of: (a) performance of a contract with you; (b) our legitimate interests in operating and improving the services; (c) compliance with a legal obligation; and (d) your consent, where applicable.

5. How we share information

Service providers. Stripe (payments), email and hosting providers, analytics, and similar vendors that act on our behalf under written agreements limiting their use of your data.

Legal and safety. When required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety of Foundry SMB, our customers, or others.

Business transfers. If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties for their marketing.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to keep the site functional, remember preferences, and measure usage. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies. Some features may not work without them.

7. Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as needed to provide the services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Account data is deleted on request, subject to legal retention requirements (typically 7 years for tax records).

8. Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. No system is perfectly secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, email support@foundrysmb.com.

California residents. Under the CCPA/CPRA, you have rights to know, delete, correct, and limit use of sensitive personal information, and to opt out of sale or sharing. We do not sell or share personal information as defined by the CPRA.

10. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

11. International transfers

We are based in the United States. If you access the services from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S.

12. Third-party links

The site may link to third-party sites and tools. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies, not this one.

13. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change.

14. Contact

Questions or requests: support@foundrysmb.com. Mailing address: Foundry SMB LLC, Colorado Springs, CO.
