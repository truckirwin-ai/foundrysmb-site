Refunds & Cancellation.

Last updated · April 30, 2026

Cancellation

You can cancel any subscription at any time by emailing billing@foundrysmb.com from the address on the account. Cancellation stops future renewals. Access continues through the end of the current paid billing cycle.

Refund terms by product

Foundry Toolkit · Monthly

Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable for the current paid month. Cancel anytime to stop the next renewal. No prorated refunds for partial months.

Foundry Toolkit · Annual

Full refund within 30 days of the initial purchase if you have not made significant downloads or use of the content (defined as more than five distinct downloads or accessing more than three premium playbooks). After 30 days, no refunds. You can still cancel renewal at any time, and access continues through the end of the paid year.

AI Revenue Sprint

Full refund if requested before kickoff. After kickoff, no refunds, because the engagement has begun and delivery work starts immediately. If Foundry cancels for its own reasons, you receive a full refund.

Operator Coaching · 1:1 and Cohort

Coaching engagements include a 90-day initial commitment. After the first month, if we are not the right fit, you may request to cancel and receive a refund of the unused portion of the 90-day commitment, prorated by month. After 90 days, coaching is month-to-month and non-refundable for the current paid month.

How to request a refund

Email billing@foundrysmb.com from the address on the account with the order number, the product, and a brief explanation. We will respond within 5 business days. Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method via Stripe and typically settle within 5 to 10 business days.

Chargebacks

Please contact us before disputing a charge with your bank. Most issues are resolved faster by email. We reserve the right to suspend access for accounts with unresolved chargebacks until the matter is resolved.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes do not affect refund eligibility for purchases made before the change.
