# Foundry SMB Sales Assets

These are the minimum assets needed to sell and deliver the AI Revenue Sprint without improvising.

## Core assets

- `ai-revenue-sprint-one-pager.md` - prospect-facing offer summary.
- `discovery-call-script.md` - first call structure and qualification prompts.
- `workflow-audit-worksheet.md` - intake worksheet for the painful workflow.
- `sprint-delivery-checklist.md` - five-day delivery checklist.
- `roi-calculator.csv` - quick math for hours, lead value, and payback.
- `sprint-to-install-proposal-template.md` - upsell proposal from sprint to system install.
- `client-handoff-template.md` - delivery closeout and ownership transfer.

## Operating rule

Every sales call should end with one of three outcomes: paid sprint, not now with a follow-up date, or no-fit closeout.
