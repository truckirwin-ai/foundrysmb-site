# AI Revenue Sprint

## Stop buying AI tools. Install the workflow that makes them pay.

The AI Revenue Sprint is a five-business-day engagement for owner-led businesses with one workflow that is costing time, money, or follow-up discipline.

**Launch price:** $1,500  
**Delivery window:** 5 business days  
**Primary buyer:** owner, operator, GM, sales lead, or department head with authority to change the workflow  
**Best workflows:** proposals, estimates, lead follow-up, reporting, SOP capture, client onboarding, inbox triage, internal knowledge search

## What you get

- Workflow diagnosis: what happens now, where work stalls, what is missing, and what should be automated or assisted.
- Opportunity map: expected time saved, revenue protected, and failure points removed.
- Prototype system: prompts, process map, lightweight automation logic, context structure, or working tool skeleton.
- Implementation backlog: what should be installed next, ranked by cash impact.
- Handoff package: checklist, SOP, prompt pack, and next-step recommendation.

## What happens in five days

1. **Day 1: Diagnose** - audit the workflow, gather examples, define the target.
2. **Day 2: Map** - document the current path and the improved operating path.
3. **Day 3: Build** - prototype the assistant, prompt chain, intake, handoff, report, or automation.
4. **Day 4: Test** - run real examples and tighten failure points.
5. **Day 5: Hand off** - deliver the working package and quote the optional install.

## What this is not

- Not generic AI coaching.
- Not a tool shopping session.
- Not a strategy deck with no working artifact.
- Not a guarantee of revenue. It is a focused build sprint aimed at a measurable workflow leak.
