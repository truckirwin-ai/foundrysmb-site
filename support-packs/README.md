# Foundry Support Packs

Support packs turn the free Toolkit into a revenue operating system.

The Toolkit helps a business owner inspect one workflow. The support packs help Foundry and the client move from inspection to pipeline, delivery, adoption, and recurring managed operations.

## Packs

| Pack | Use when | Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Lead Capture and CRM Pack](lead-capture-crm-pack.md) | Toolkit traffic needs to become qualified pipeline | Form fields, routing rules, CRM stages, email follow-up, source tracking |
| [Founder-Led Sales Sprint Pack](founder-led-sales-sprint-pack.md) | Foundry needs conversations now | Outreach cadence, lead scoring, call flow, objection handling, daily scoreboard |
| [Managed Ops Onboarding Pack](managed-ops-onboarding-pack.md) | A sprint/install should turn into recurring revenue | Client onboarding, monthly cadence, backlog, review gates, reporting |
| [Content Cash Loop Pack](content-cash-loop-pack.md) | Toolkit insights need to become traffic and trust | Teardown posts, short video scripts, lead magnet CTAs, repurposing workflow |
| [Client Measurement Pack](client-measurement-pack.md) | The client needs proof the workflow is paying | KPI definitions, before/after capture, weekly scorecard, renewal proof |

## Operating rule

Every support pack must connect to one of four cash outcomes:

- more qualified leads
- more paid sprints
- more system installs
- more recurring managed ops

If a support activity does not support one of those outcomes, it is probably noise.
