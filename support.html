S.2 · Frequently asked

Common questions.

How do I change or cancel a paid engagement?

Email billing@foundrysmb.com from the address on the account. We confirm the change in writing and apply the terms in the signed order, statement of work, or Refunds & Cancellation policy.

What payment methods do you accept?

Invoices, ACH, and major cards are available depending on the engagement. Foundry does not store card information.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, with terms specific to each product. See the Refunds & Cancellation Policy.

Where is my receipt or invoice?

Receipts and invoices are sent to the billing email on file. If you cannot find one, email billing@foundrysmb.com.

Can I change scope?

Yes. Sprint, install, coaching, and managed operations scope changes are handled in writing so deliverables, timing, and payment terms stay clear.

Is the Toolkit content downloadable?

Yes. Toolkit resources are free to use inside one business. Do not resell, repackage, or publicly repost them as your own.

Can I share access with my team?

Yes, inside the same business. For use across multiple businesses, client delivery, or resale, contact hello@foundrysmb.com.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email support@foundrysmb.com. We remove personal data per the Privacy Policy. Some records may be retained for tax, contractual, and legal compliance.
